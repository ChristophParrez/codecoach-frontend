CodeCoach aims to facilitate the peer-to-peer coaching sessions between professional programmers. Developers can register as coachee or coach and ask for support to grow: Using a coaching session, via a code review, ...

CodeCoach is an academic coaching program between students in secondary schools, supervised by the schools.

Students with difficulties in a subject and who want to make progress can ask for explanations and advice to other students in their school who have mastered the subject or have already acquired a good working method.

With CodeCoach everything is quick and easy: just register as a Coach or request a coaching session on this website, and it will organize a private coaching session in the school.

Our digital platform has the following features:

  • Register
  • Request coaching or code review
  • Provide coaching or code review
  • Provide feedback
